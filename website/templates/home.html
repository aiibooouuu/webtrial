Services

Roadmap

Our expert instructors have carefully crafted personalized roadmaps for each course. These roadmaps guide you through the learning journey, highlighting key milestones, essential resources, and recommended activities. As you progress, your instructor will provide feedback and support to ensure you're on track to achieve your goals.

UI/UX Design

We've meticulously crafted our UI/UX to ensure a seamless and enjoyable learning experience for both instructors and students. Our intuitive interface makes it effortless to navigate through courses, access resources, and track progress. Whether you're an instructor designing a course or a student eager to learn, Tick My Way is designed with your needs in mind.

currency system

As you tick your way through the course, you'll earn XP points, propelling you towards higher levels. Each level unlocks new features, resources, and rewards, keeping you motivated and excited to continue your learning journey. Once you reach certain milestones or complete significant sections of the course, you'll be rewarded with Platinum, our exclusive in-app currency. Platinum can be used to unlock premium features, purchase exclusive content, or customize your profile and avatar.

Upcoming Features

One of our most exciting upcoming features is our AI-powered test generator. This groundbreaking tool will create customized tests tailored to your specific learning style and progress. However, to unlock this powerful feature, you'll need to earn a certain number of Platinum, our exclusive in-app currency. Additionally, we're developing an AI-powered transcription feature that will automatically generate notes from uploaded videos or PDFs. This will be a valuable tool for last-minute study or revision. However, to access this feature, you'll also need to earn a specific amount of Platinum.
