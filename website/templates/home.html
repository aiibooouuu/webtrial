Chartered Accountant


Foundation Course:
Eligibility: Complete Class 12 or equivalent with a minimum of 55% marks.
Subjects: Accounting, Law, Economics, and Business Mathematics.
Exam: Clear the Foundation exam conducted by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI).

Intermediate Course:
Eligibility: Clear the Foundation exam.
Groups: The course is divided into two groups: Group 1 and Group 2.
Subjects: Accounting, Auditing, Law, Taxation, Cost Accounting, and Financial Management.
Exams: Clear both Group 1 and Group 2 exams.

Final Course:
Eligibility: Clear both groups of the Intermediate course.
Modules: The course is divided into four modules: Auditing, Taxation, Corporate and Allied Laws, and Financial Reporting.
Articleship: Complete a three-year articleship training under a qualified CA.

Exams: Clear all four modules of the Final exam.
